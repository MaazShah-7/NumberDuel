# Number Duel - User Guide

Welcome to Number Duel! This guide will help you understand how to register, play matches, and use private rooms.

## 1. Getting Started
When you open the app for the first time, you will see the **Login** screen.
- If you are a new user, tap "Don't have an account? Register".
- Enter a unique **Username** and a **Password**.
- Once registered, you will automatically log in. 
- Your account is now permanent! You start with **1000 Coins**.

## 2. Your Profile
On the Home screen, tap your **Profile Card** at the top to view your stats:
- **Coins:** Your current balance. Used for match entry fees.
- **Matches Played / Won / Lost:** Your permanent record.
- **Edit Profile:** You can change your Profile Picture (using an Emoji like 🦊 or a URL), your Username, or your Password.

## 3. Playing a Game
Before playing, you must choose a **Number Range** (e.g., 100 means you guess a number between 1 and 100). The default is 100.

### Option A: Join Random Match
- Tap **Join Random Match**.
- The app will find another player who wants to play with the **exact same range**.
- Once matched, the game begins! (Entry fee: 100 Coins).

### Option B: Play with Friends (Private Room)
- **To Host:** Enter your desired Range and tap **Create Private Room**. The app will generate a secure **7-digit code**. Share this code with your friend.
- **To Join:** Your friend must type the 7-digit code into the bottom text box and tap **Join**.

## 4. The Rules of the Duel
1. **Setup Phase:** Both players secretly choose a "Target Number" within the agreed range and lock it in.
2. **Battle Phase:** Players take turns guessing the opponent's secret number.
3. **Hints:** After a guess, the game will tell both players if the actual target number is **Higher** or **Lower** than the guess.
4. **Winning:** The first player to guess the exact number wins the pot (200 Coins)!
